Boom Truck Usage in Saudi Arabia

Boom trucks are widely used across industries for tasks that require lifting, transporting, and placing heavy loads. Commonly seen on construction sites, they are ideal for lifting steel beams, HVAC units, and building materials. In the oil and gas sector, they assist with equipment installation and pipeline maintenance. Utility companies use them for pole and transformer placement.

Boom trucks are also valuable in sign installation, tree trimming, and infrastructure repair, thanks to their ability to reach high or hard-to-access areas quickly and safely with minimal setup.
